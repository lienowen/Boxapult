# Route Design

## Route 1 — Downtown Dash

Purpose: teach movement, firing, parcel interception and the survival objective.

- 45 seconds
- 6 required parcels
- majority scout enemies
- wide fairness window
- first power-up arrives after the player understands the base loop

## Route 2 — Harbor Express

Purpose: increase target pressure and introduce more raider fire without changing controls.

- 52 seconds
- 8 required parcels
- faster world movement
- more raiders and interceptors
- slightly faster parcel cadence so difficulty comes from prioritization, not starvation

## Route 3 — Midnight Priority

Purpose: mastery test.

- 60 seconds
- 10 required parcels
- highest enemy density
- interceptor and raider majority
- shorter power-up interval to support aggressive play

## Fairness rules

- The game never requires shooting a parcel.
- Parcel spawn cadence leaves at least a 35% miss allowance over the target.
- Enemy bullets aim at the player's current position, not a predicted future position.
- Damage grants a short invulnerability window.
- A shield absorbs one complete impact.
- Missing an enemy lowers combo slightly; missing a parcel resets it, creating a clear delivery priority.
