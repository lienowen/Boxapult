## Phaser's Revenge demo structure

Parcel Patrol's scene flow and basic arcade-shooter organization were adapted from the official **Phaser's Revenge** demo distributed in the `phaserjs/create-game` repository.

- Source repository: https://github.com/phaserjs/create-game
- Source path: `scaffolding/demo/phasers-revenge`
- License declared by the source package: MIT

The source demo's artwork, bitmap fonts, music, and sound assets were not copied into Parcel Patrol. Current Parcel Patrol visuals are generated by `src/runtime/parcelPatrol/ArtFactory.ts`; current sound effects are synthesized by `src/runtime/parcelPatrol/ToneSfx.ts`.
